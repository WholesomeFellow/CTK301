The Final Will and Testimony of Dr. Ian

This is a short fiction piece I wrote for a creative writing workshop, I tried experimenting with how text is displayed and the obstruction of it. The story itself is framed as a government document and is the final letter written by a scientist who unknowingly contributed to the coming apocolypse.

The Wall Must Fall

This piece is a short story I wrote independently to try and explore the cyberpunk genre. This was originally inspired by Saudi Arabia's project "The Line", and follows a worker using their cybernetic enhancements to build the project before ultimately deciding to rebel against the project and what it stands for.
