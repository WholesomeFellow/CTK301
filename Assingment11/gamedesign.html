Bugged and sorted

Bugged and sorted is a small game I was a part of creating for a Univeristy game jam, where it placed second. The goal of the game is that you are a factory worker who has to organize different robotic bugs constantly being delievered to the player on conveyor belts and throw away bad ones.

My contributions

  • Project Managment, done using Kanban methodology
  • Concepting
  • Audio Design

Ghosts N' Guys

This was the final class project for my first game design course. In this board game, up to four players are tasked with exploring a haunted hotel, while a 5th player acts as the ghost, ruining the plans of the other players.

My contributions

  • Project managment and organization
  • Writing the story
  • Rules and rulebook editing
